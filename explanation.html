Instructions

Surveys
You will first complete three surveys: the Short Schwartz Value Survey (SSVS), the Positive and Negative Affect Schedule (PANAS), and the State-Trait Anxiety Inventory for Adults (STAI-S). These surveys will help us understand your values, current emotional state, and anxiety levels.

Game Instructions
After completing the surveys, you will participate in a game with other online participants. The rules of the game will be explained to you after the completion of the surveys.

At the end of the experiment, you will be asked to complete the PANAS and STAI-S surveys again.
